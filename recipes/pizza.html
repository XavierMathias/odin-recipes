Pizza

This pizza was inspired from and old, italian man from the outskirts of Venice, Italy. Taught down through generations, he became an expert in the ingredients he puts together

Like fresh romaine tomatos, mazzoralla cheese, fresh bread, and new herbs

Recipe

  • Dough
  • Tomatos
  • Cheese
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Put the dough in the oven
  3. Meanwhile, squash tomato sauce
  4. Take out the cooked bread and apply the fresh tomato sauce
  5. Add cheese and rest of the ingredients
