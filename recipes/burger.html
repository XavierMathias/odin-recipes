Burger

Delicious burger made with fresh ingredients

I will destroy something so good like this juicy piece of culinary art!

Recipes

  • Burger Buns
  • Tomatos
  • Cheese
  • Beef Patty

Steps

  1. Cut tomatos into slices
  2. Sear the beef patty until well cooked
  3. Put cheese on top of the cooked patty
  4. Toast the buns
  5. Place the patty and melted cheese on top of the bottom bun
  6. Place tomatos on top and close with a top bun
