Tacos

Bringing the mexican culture int your own home is the best way to enjoy a delicious meal without having to leave your own house. And it's easy to make!

with a crunchy taco shell, beefy taco meat, fresh veggies, and a secret sauce that takes it to another level

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatos
  • Lettuce
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Cut the lettuce and tomatos
  2. Cook the ground beef with condiments of your liking
  3. Preheat a pan, and once hot place a tortilla on top. Flip once it is golden brown
  4. Place the ground beef on the middle of the tortilla
  5. Place the lettuce, tomatos, and sour cream on top
